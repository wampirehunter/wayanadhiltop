Must-Visit Locations in Wayanad

Explore the best of what Wayanad has to offer

Banasura Sagar Dam

The largest earth dam in India and the second largest in Asia. A perfect spot for boating and enjoying the scenic views of the surrounding hills.

View on Map

Edakkal Caves

Known for its prehistoric rock carvings, this cave offers a challenging trek with a rewarding view from the top. A must-visit for history and adventure lovers.

View on Map

Soochipara Waterfalls

A mesmerizing three-tiered waterfall surrounded by dense evergreen forests. It's a great spot for trekking and enjoying the cool, refreshing water.

View on Map

Lakkidi View Point

Offering a breathtaking panoramic view of the 'ghat' road with its nine hairpin bends. A great spot for a stunning sunrise or sunset view.

View on Map

Pookode Lake

A beautiful freshwater lake surrounded by evergreen forests. Activities like boating and exploring the freshwater aquarium are popular here.

View on Map

Karlad Lake

A tranquil lake ideal for adventure enthusiasts, offering zip-lining, kayaking, and boating. A perfect place for a day of fun and activities.

View on Map
