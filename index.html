About Us

Nature's Bounty at Hill Top Agro-Farm Resort

Hill Top Agro Farm Resort is an environmentally friendly green stay that provides perfect solitude to people seeking a retreat into nature. Located in Wayanad, on 10 acres of greenery with a mountain view and a variety of agro crops.

A place where you are with a peaceful location from the city crowd integrated with a mountain view, variety of agri crops, peaceful atmosphere, nature walk trek and much more.

  • Nearest Airport – Kannur
  • Nearest Railway Station – Vadakara
  • Nearest multi speciality hospitals around 10km
